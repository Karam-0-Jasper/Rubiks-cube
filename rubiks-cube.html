Rubik's Cube

Learn the basics before you start solving

1

The Goal

Each face of the cube has 9 stickers. You need to get every sticker on a face to be the same color — 6 faces, 6 solid colors.

White (Top)
Yellow (Bottom)
Red (Front)
Orange (Back)
Blue (Left)
Green (Right)
2

Making Moves

Click the move buttons below the cube. Each letter is a face: U (Up), D (Down), F (Front), B (Back), L (Left), R (Right). A plain letter = clockwise. The ' (prime) symbol = counter-clockwise.

4

Rotate the View

Click and drag the cube to spin it around and see all sides. You can't solve what you can't see.
